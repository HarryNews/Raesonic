Raesonic Privacy Policy

Revision Date: Jul 6, 2016
  • Introduction

    Raesonic ("Raesonic" or the "Site") is a web application provided to you by the Raesonic team ("we", "our" or "us"). Raesonic respects your privacy and the confidentiality of your personal information, and takes steps in order to help you maintain it. This Privacy Policy describes how we deal with your personal information. It is important that you read and understand our policies. We reserve the right to change, alter, replace or otherwise modify the Privacy Policy. The date of last modification is stated at the beginning of the document. Make sure to check the Privacy Policy for updates as we might have to improve or adapt it in the future.

    By using the Site and its services, and in particular by creating a User Account, you are consenting to the use of your information in the manner set out in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to any of the provisions of this Privacy Policy, you should not use the Site or the services it provides.
  • Personal Information We Collect

    When you create a User Account, we ask you to choose a username and a password. To verify the account, you will have to provide your email address. We will store the date you signed up on, and the day you last visited the Site on. If you choose to supply the profile fields, such as real name, country you live in, or a short biography to describe yourself, that information will be stored as well. You will be able to modify and delete those profile fields at a later time.
  • How We Use Collected Information

    We use the information that we collect about you for the following purposes:
    • To operate and maintain your User Account, and to provide you with access to the Site and any services that you may request from time to time. Your username and password are used to identify you when you sign into the Site.
    • To provide you with technical support, and to respond to your comments and enquiries.
    • To provide other users with data regarding user profiles, public playlists and changes.
    • Your email address will be used to send you service updates and important notices regarding your account, account recovery links, and any notification emails you opted in to.
    • To analyze the use of the Site, and the people using its services, in order to improve the quality and adapt the Site to your needs and interests.
    • To prevent or take action against activities that are, or may be, in breach of our Terms of Service or applicable law.
    For other purposes, provided we disclose this to you at the relevant time, and provided that you agree to the proposed use of your personal data.
  • Cookies and Local Storage

    Third-Party Content providers may serve advertisements based on your cookies. These cookies allow to display ads based on your visits to the Site and other sites that use similar advertising services. We do not share your personal information with the Third-Party Content providers, other than information they can obtain from the request messages, such as cookies and IP addresses. Tracking done by the Third-Party Content providers through cookies is subject to their own privacy policies.

    Information about the recently viewed playlists, and certain player settings, including, without limitation, player volume and the playback quality setting, will be stored on your computer to improve the quality of the services we provide.
  • Law Enforcement

    We may disclose your information to a third party where we believe, in good faith that we are required to for legal purposes, in case of a criminal investigation, or a civil subpoena. If we receive such a request we may, but are not required to, notify you of such request and give you an opportunity to respond.
  • Children

    Anyone under the age of 13 is not permitted to use the Site and must not attempt to create a User Account or submit any personal information to us. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from any person who is under the age of 13 or allow them to create an account. If it comes to our attention that we have collected personal data from a person under the age of 13, we will delete this information as quickly as possible. If you have reason to believe that we may have collected any such personal data, please notify us immediately.
